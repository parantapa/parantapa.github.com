Parantapa Bhattacharya

I am a PhD Scholar at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. I work with Prof. Niloy Ganguly and Prof. S. K. Ghosh. My work involves using minimal amounts of network analysis, statistics, and machine learning to study online social networks. I am also part of the Complex Networks Research Group.

You can contact me at pb [at] parantapa [dot] net.
